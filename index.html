When to Take Your Maternity Photography Session

The Perfect Timing

The sweet spot for maternity photography is usually between 28 and 34 weeks of pregnancy. At this stage your belly has a lovely, round shape that beautifully shows your pregnancy glow. You still have enough energy to move comfortably, pose, and enjoy the session. It’s early enough to avoid the last few weeks, when you might feel more tired or uncomfortable.

Read more

Too Early (before 24 weeks): Your bump may not be fully visible in photos yet. Too Late (after 36 weeks): You may feel more tired, and there’s always the chance your little one could decide to arrive early.

Because maternity sessions are best done within that 28–34 week window, it’s a good idea to book your session early, ideally in your second trimester. This gives us plenty of time to plan your outfits, location, and style so you feel relaxed and prepared.

Indoor or Outdoor Photoshoot – Which is Right for You?

One of the first decisions you’ll make when booking your photoshoot is where it should take place – indoors or outdoors. Both options have their unique charm, and the choice really depends on your style, comfort, and the story you want your photos to tell.

Read more

Indoor Photoshoots – Comfort & Control

Indoor sessions are ideal if you’re looking for a more intimate, cozy, or controlled environment. Weather-proof: No stress about rain, heat, or unpredictable winds. Comfort for little ones: Perfect for newborns or young children who may feel more relaxed at home or in a studio.

Outdoor Photoshoots – Natural Beauty & Adventure

Outdoor sessions give your photos a sense of openness and connection to nature. Golden hour magic: Sunrise and sunset bring soft, flattering light that’s hard to beat. Kids can run, play, and explore — leading to natural, joyful expressions.

Lifestyle Newborn Photography — Capturing Your First Days Together

Welcoming a new baby into the world is such a magical, emotional time. Those first days are filled with love, tiny details, and moments you’ll want to remember forever.

FAQ

How do I book a session? +
You can book directly through my contact form or by sending me a message. I’ll discuss your preferred date, location, and style, and I’ll guide you through the process.
What should we wear for a family photoshoot? +
I recommend neutral or soft colors that complement each other. Avoid large logos or distracting patterns. I provide a full styling guide after booking.
Where will the photoshoot take place? +
We can shoot outdoors in a natural location, at your home for a lifestyle feel, or in a studio if preferred.
How long will the session take? +
Most family sessions last 45–90 minutes. Newborn sessions are 1.5–3 hours depending on the session type.
Do you work with small children? +
Absolutely. I have patience and tricks to keep little ones engaged — the best photos come from real moments, not forced poses.
When will we receive our photos? +
Within 3–5 days you'll receive an unedited gallery to choose favourites; edited gallery ready within two weeks after selections.
